FINCA
SERENITY

San Rafael, Ibiza
I. The Sanctuary

The Arrival

Crossing the bridge over the water garden, the pace of the outside world drops away.

Ancient stone, quiet water, and a home grounded in simplicity. It is an entrance that doesn't demand attention, but offers immediate, absolute calm.

Where the outside world ends and yours begins.

Inside & Out

At Finca Serenity, the boundaries between the home and the island simply fall away.

Floor-to-ceiling doors remain open, pulling the Mediterranean light and the scent of pine directly into the rooms. The home is designed to gather people — generously, without effort.

Understated in every detail.

The Heart of the Home

The farmhouse kitchen centres around a tactile sage island, designed for slow mornings with coffee and open-ended evenings.

The walk-in fridge and pantry are stocked before you arrive. Whether you gather to cook or leave it entirely to your private chef, this is where the day begins and where it always returns.

Where mornings begin and evenings refuse to end.

Finca to Table

The garden gives quietly and generously. Rosemary pulled by hand. Tomatoes still warm from the afternoon sun. Lemons from the grove, split open at the table.

There is something deeply grounding about a meal that begins where you are standing. Whether your private chef takes the lead or you wander the beds yourself, the distance from soil to plate is measured in footsteps.

Grown here. Eaten here.

Elemental Living

The truest luxury is space, silence, and a connection to nature.

A hand-carved stone bathtub sits quietly on the upper terrace — an invitation to bathe under the Ibiza sky. The home is defined by these quiet, unexpected luxuries.

Nothing between you and the sky.

II. The Life

Dining

Feasts that span hours, set against the backdrop of the olive groves. Whether it's a sunrise breakfast on the terrace or a candlelit dinner under the pergola, dining here is an act of connection.

Fresh, local ingredients, prepared with care, and served in the open air where time seems to move differently.

The table is set. The rest writes itself.

Al Fresco

Every meal finds its own setting.

A long table beneath the pergola for evening feasts. The stone terrace for lazy lunches. The olive grove for a blanket and a bottle of something cold. The kitchen garden for those who want to pick before they plate.

No walls. No rules. No rush.

Art & Culture

Walls change with the seasons.

Finca Serenity is curated in partnership with Corinthian Contemporary Art — an internationally recognised foundation that rotates original works throughout the home. Each stay reveals a new collection.

The walls have something to say.

The Pool

The pool sits at the heart of the estate — designed for leisurely dips amid birdsong, not laps.

Pink loungers line the stone deck. A shaded cabana offers refuge from the afternoon sun. Staff bring drinks; nobody expects you anywhere.

Be still, and the day finds you.

Still Water

The day arranges itself around the water. A game of croquet on the lower lawn. The cricket net for those who need to bowl a few overs. The pickleball court for something more competitive.

Or simply stay exactly where you are. The cabana is shaded, the drinks are cold, and the afternoon stretches on.

Nowhere to be. Nothing to prove.

The Grounds

Beyond the pool, 25,000 square metres of terraced gardens, olive groves and pine forest unfold across the hillside.

A treehouse hidden in the pines with its own romantic ensuite. Two secluded yurts for those drawn to sleep beneath the stars — or equally suited as private staff quarters, set apart from the main house.

Twenty-five thousand reasons to wander.

III. The Night

After Dark

Beneath a ceiling of stars, your own private club.

Full bar. Professional stage. Sound system throughout. Bring your own DJ. Hire a band. Or simply turn the lights down, open the bar, and let the evening find its own rhythm.

Come for a drink. Stay for the dawn.

The Stage

The stage is ready. The guitars are waiting. The only question is who plays first.

Pick up a guitar. Or just listen.

IV. The Retreat

Wellness

Sauna. Steam room. Ice plunge. A wellness circuit designed for recovery — or simply for the ritual of it.

A fully equipped gym. A yoga deck overlooking the groves. Every path to stillness, in one place.

Slow down, and the body remembers.

The Steam Room

Warmth built into the walls. Step from the cold plunge into a cocoon of heat and handmade mosaic — and let the island fall away entirely.

Let the heat do the thinking.

V. Places to Rest

The Bedrooms

Seven en-suite bedrooms, a private apartment with its own kitchen and living space, and a standalone studio — each with its own character, all sharing the same quiet comfort.

High beamed ceilings, cool stone floors, and French doors that open to the sound of the cicadas. A place of retreat, where the thickness of the walls keeps the midday heat at bay.

Sleep deeply. Wake slowly.

Quiet Details

Each bedroom opens to its own private bathroom — cool stone, natural light, and the unhurried feeling of a home that has thought of everything.

Luxury lives in the things left unsaid.

The Apartment

A self-contained retreat on the lower ground floor — two en-suite bedrooms, its own living space and kitchen, with direct access to the gardens below.

Complete independence for extended family, older children, or those who simply prefer their own rhythm.

A home within the home.

The Hideaways

Secret spaces to escape the world.

The Treehouse, suspended in the pines for a night amongst the branches. The Bodega, stocked and waiting for late-night conversations.

A place to lose yourself, and find yourself again.

The Rhythm
of a Day

No itinerary.
Just possibility.
Dawn
Sunlight through the shutters. Espresso on the terrace. The island stirs beneath you.
Morning
A pickleball rally on the court. A cool dip. The children have already found the treehouse.
Midday
Lunch under the pergola — grilled catch of the day, salads pulled from the garden, local rosé. Nobody checks the time.
Afternoon
Some retreat to the pool. Others wander the orchards. The sauna is warm. The hammock is waiting.
Dusk
The sky turns pink, then gold. The bay of San Antonio glimmers in the distance. No one moves.
Evening
Dinner outside — platters, candles, bare feet on warm stone. Someone opens the bar. The stage is set.
Late
The telescope comes out. Jupiter, the rings of Saturn, the Sea of Tranquility. Tomorrow writes itself.
