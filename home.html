The Estate

Where the outside world drops away

Hidden within terraced gardens and ancient pines, where Phoenicians first cultivated the valley, Finca Serenity is a home built for a life well lived.

One filled with people, long lunches, games on the lawn, music in the evenings, and quiet moments of calm. The house has the warmth of a traditional Ibicencan finca, with the space and privacy to do whatever you please.

Stay, host, move, rest — and never feel the need to leave.

A Day at Finca Serenity

From sunrise to stars

8:00 AM

Wake to sunlight and the scent of pine. Step onto the terrace with an espresso as the island slowly stirs.

10:00 AM

Rally the family for a pickleball match, followed by a cool dip. The children have already found the treehouse.

1:00 PM

Lunch in the pool pergola. Grilled catch of the day, salads from the garden, local rosé. Nobody checks the time.

4:00 PM

A lazy afternoon. Some retreat to the pool. Others wander the orchards. The sauna is there for those who want it.

7:00 PM

Sunset ritual. The sky blushes pink, then gold. The bay of San Antonio twinkles in the distance.

10:00 PM

Dinner outside — platters, candles, bare feet on warm stone. Then the club. Someone opens the bar.

Midnight

The Celestron telescope is brought out. Jupiter is visible. Tomorrow can be as full or as free as you choose.
