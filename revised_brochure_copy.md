**Title:** FINCA SERENITY

## I. THE SANCTUARY

### Page 2: The Arrival
**Visual:** `finca-bridge.jpg` (Water Garden Bridge)
**Copy:**
Crossing the bridge over the water garden, the pace of the outside world drops away.

Ancient stone, quiet water, and a home grounded in simplicity. It is an entrance that doesn’t demand attention, but offers immediate, absolute calm.

### Page 3: Inside & Out
**Visual:** `finca-inside-out.jpg` (Open Living Room)
**Copy:**
At Finca Serenity, the boundaries between the home and the island simply fall away.

Floor-to-ceiling doors remain open, pulling the Mediterranean light and the scent of pine directly into the rooms. The home is built for generous hosting, but never feels overwhelming.

*Elegant, but never showy.*

### Page 4: The Kitchen
**Visual:** `finca-kitchen.jpg` (Kitchen Island)
**Copy:**
The farmhouse kitchen centres around a tactile sage island, designed for slow mornings with coffee and open-ended evenings.

The walk-in fridge and pantry arrive fully stocked. Whether you gather to cook or leave it entirely to your private chef, it is the effortless heart of the home.

### Page 5: Elemental Living
**Visual:** `finca-bathtub.jpg` (Stone Bathtub)
**Copy:**
The truest luxury is space, silence, and a connection to nature.

A hand-carved stone bathtub sits quietly on the upper terrace—an invitation to bathe under the Ibiza sky. The home is defined by these quiet, unexpected luxuries.

## II. THE LIFE

### Page 6: Dining
**Visual:** `finca-dining.jpg` (Pergola Table)
**Copy:**
Gather under the pergola. Simple pleasures. The clinking of glasses. The warmth of the stone.

### Page 7: Art & Culture
**Visual:** `charles-9.jpg` (Art Piece)
**Copy:**
Walls change with the seasons.

Finca Serenity is curated in partnership with Corinthian Contemporary Art—an internationally recognised art foundation that rotates original works.

### Page 8: The Pool
**Visual:** `charles-10.jpg` (Pool Long Shot)
**Copy:**
The pool sits at the heart of the estate—designed for leisurely dips amid birdsong, not laps.

Pink loungers line the stone deck. A shaded cabana offers refuge from the afternoon sun. Staff bring drinks; nobody expects you anywhere.

### Page 9: The Grounds
**Visual:** `BLUE HOUR-1.jpg` (Grounds/Tennis/Games)
**Copy:**
Beyond the pool, 25,000 square metres of terraced gardens, olives and pine forest sit waiting to be explored.

For play: A cricket net with professional bowling machine, a professional pickleball court, and croquet on the lower lawn.

Play badminton amongst the olives—or set up a table in their shade and lose an afternoon.

### Page 10: The Hideaways
**Visual:** `finca-treehouse.jpg` (Treehouse)
**Copy:**
For escape: Hidden in the woods, an elevated treehouse with a romantic ensuite, and two secluded yurts for those who want to sleep closer to nature.

*A place to lose yourself, and find yourself again.*

## III. THE NIGHT

### Page 11: After Dark
**Visual:** `finca-club.jpg` (Club Interior)
**Copy:**
Beneath a ceiling of stars, your own private club.

Full bar. Professional stage. Sound system throughout. Bring your own DJ. Hire a band. Or simply turn the lights down, open the bar, and let the evening find its own rhythm.

### Page 12: The Stage
**Visual:** `finca-stage.jpg` (Music Stage)
**Copy:**
The stage is ready. The guitars are waiting. The only question is who plays first.

*The night becomes whatever you want it to be.*
