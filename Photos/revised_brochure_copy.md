# PAGE 2: THE ESTATE

**Visual:** `charles-35.jpg`

Hidden within terraced gardens and ancient pines, where ancient Phoenicians first cultivated the valley, Finca Serenity is a home built for a life well lived.

One filled with people, long lunches, games on the lawn, music in the evenings, and quiet moments of calm. The house has the warmth of a traditional Ibicencan finca, with the space and privacy to do whatever you please.

Finca Serenity invites you to stay, host, move, rest, and never feel the need to leave.

*7 bedrooms | 16 guests | 25,000 sq.m estate*

# PAGE 15: A DAY AT FINCA SERENITY

**8:00 AM** — Wake to sunlight and the scent of pine. Step onto the terraces with an espresso as the island slowly stirs.

**10:00 AM** — Rally the family for a pickleball match, followed by a cool dip. The children have already found the treehouse.

**1:00 PM** — Lunch in the swimming pool pergola. Grilled catch of the day, salads from the garden, local rosé. Nobody checks the time.

**4:00 PM** — A lazy afternoon. Some retreat to the pool. Others wander the orchards and groves. The sauna is there for those who want it.

**7:00 PM** — Sunset ritual. The sky blushes pink, then gold. The bay of San Antonio twinkles in the distance.

**10:00 PM** — Dinner outside—platters, candles, bare feet on warm stone. Then the club. Someone opens the bar.

**Midnight** — The Celestron telescope is brought out. Jupiter is visible, the rings of Saturn or the sea of tranquility on the moon. Tomorrow can be as full or as free as you choose.
