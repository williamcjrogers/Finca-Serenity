The Main House

3 En-suite Bedrooms Master Terrace Main Floor

Three en-suite bedrooms, including the master suite on the upper terrace. High beamed ceilings, cool stone floors, and French doors that open to the sound of the cicadas.

A place of quiet retreat, where the thickness of the walls keeps the midday heat at bay.

The Apartment

2 En-suite Bedrooms Private Living & Kitchen Garden Access

Two en-suite bedrooms with private living space and kitchen. Located on the lower ground floor, it offers complete independence and direct access to the lower gardens.

Perfect for extended family or guests who value their own rhythm.

The Studio

Double & Bunk Beds Kitchenette Standalone

A standalone hideaway with double bed and bunk beds—ideal for families. An architecturally beautiful space with its own kitchenette and en-suite, separate from the main house.

Intimate, functional, and full of character.
